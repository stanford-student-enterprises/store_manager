Store Manager

Welcome to the student store inventory manager!

Instructions

Click on Compare to see the inventories for the common items between the two stores.

Click on Store Inventory to view the inventory of items listed in VendHQ.

Click on Online Inventory to view the inventory of items listed in BigCommerce.

Click on the SKU of any item to view its global inventory.

When on a page for a single item, click "Edit" to set the actual inventory count of that item. This will immediately reset the inventory in both stores, and use this value to track future inventory.
